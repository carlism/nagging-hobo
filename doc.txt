Requesting a new scheduled job looks like this:

POST https://momentapp.com/jobs.json?
  job[at]=2011-01-31T18:36:21&
  job[method]=POST&
  job[uri]=http://yourapp.com/?var1=true&var2=false&
  apikey=[your_api_key]
